# Lidar C1 360

* AVOID_ENABLE 3
* AVOID_BEHAVE 1 stop
* AVOID_DIST_MAX 2 
* AVOID_MARGIN 2

* SERIAL1_PROTOCOL = “11” (“Lidar360”)
* SERIAL1_BAUD = “115” for C1/A2, “256” for S1,A2M12
* PRX1_TYPE = “5”
* PRX1_ORIENT = “0” if mounted on the top of the vehicle, “1” if mounted upside-down on the bottom of the vehicle.
